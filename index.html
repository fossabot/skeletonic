Meet the sleek, lightweight, intuitive and ultra-responsive CSS framework.

Dead on

All you have to do is drop Skeletonic into your project, and everything will be styled automatically. There is a minimal amount of classes to learn.

Lightweight

Skeletonic comes packed with features, including styling for most elements, a grid, and some components, all in just 5kb.

Responsive

The styles that come with Skeletonic are clean and modern, dropping it into your project will give you a headstart in design.

Responsive

Simple, responsive grid that collapses at small viewport widths.
